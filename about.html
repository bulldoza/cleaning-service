Our Story

We Clean Like It's Our Own Home

Sparkle Clean started as a simple idea — that a cleaning service should be someone you actually trust, not a stranger who shows up and disappears.

Why We Started

Tired of Excuses. Built for Accountability.

Like most Charlotte homeowners, we spent years cycling through cleaning services that looked great on paper but let us down in the home. Missed spots, no-shows, and zero follow-through when we raised concerns. We figured we weren't the only ones.

So we built Sparkle Clean from the ground up — an owner-operated, background-checked, fully insured service where accountability isn't a policy, it's personal. When something's not right, we don't redirect you to a call center. We come back and fix it ourselves.

We serve Charlotte and the surrounding communities because this is where we live. These are our neighbors' homes, and we treat them that way.

— The Sparkle Clean Team

How We Work

Three Things We Never Compromise

Every decision we make comes back to these.

01

Accountability

We stand behind every clean with our 48-hour re-clean guarantee. If you're not satisfied, we come back — no questions, no runaround.

02

Transparency

No surprise charges, no hidden fees, no franchise fine print. You get a clear quote upfront and a consistent team every time.

03

Trust

Every cleaner is background-checked, reference-verified, and trained before entering a client's home. We'd only send someone we'd trust in our own house.

What You Can Count On

Every booking, every time.

Insured & Bonded

Every Sparkle team is fully insured and bonded in North Carolina. Your home and belongings are always protected.

Background-Checked

Every cleaner passes a thorough background check before their first booking. We wouldn't send anyone to your home that we wouldn't trust in our own.

48-Hour Guarantee

Not satisfied with any part of the clean? Contact us within 48 hours and we'll send a team back to fix it for free.

Eco-Friendly Products

We use non-toxic, EPA-certified cleaning products that are safe for your children, pets, and the environment.

Owner-Operated

The person who cares most about your experience is the one showing up. We're not a franchise — your satisfaction is personal to us.

Consistent Teams

We assign you the same cleaning team whenever possible so you build a relationship with people you know and trust.
